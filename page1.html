Sun in Gemini

The Twins

Gemini is the third astralogical sign in the zodiac, representing the twins Pollux and Castor. Those born between May 21st and June 21st are considered geminis. The element associated with this sign is air, and it is ruled by the planet Mercury. it is a positive mutable sign and is associated with intelligence, communication and playflness.

The sun sign represents ones core self, their inherent values and the way in which they navigate the world.
